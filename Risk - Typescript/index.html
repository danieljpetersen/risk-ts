Risk (How to play)

3 Armies Left To Assign

Move/Attack With Entire Army (Press 1, 2, or 3 to change)

How To Play

  • You're the color blue.
  • At the start of every turn you get armies to assign. Click on a territory you own until you've assigned all armies.
  • Hold shift down to assign 10 armies per click.
  • Once done assigning territories, click on a territory to select it.
  • When a territory is selected, click on a neighboring territory to attack/move.
  • Press enter to advance turn.
  • Capture at least one territory per turn to earn a card
  • There are three card types. If you accumulate one of each card, you get an income bonus of 15 for that turn. If you accumulate 3 of one card, you get an income bonus of 7.
  • Owning all of a continent will give you also give you an income bonus. Additionally, you get an extra 1 army per every 7 territories you own.
  • Goal is to conquer the world. Good luck!
